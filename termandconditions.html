Terms & Conditions

Welcome to WorkHub — a digital platform that connects Customers, Companies, Workers, and Freelancers to make hiring, service delivery, and payments smooth and transparent. By using our services, you agree to the following Terms and Conditions.

Platform Overview

WorkHub acts as a bridge between customers and service providers (workers, companies, or freelancers). The platform allows users to:

  • Book professional services directly from verified workers or companies.
  • Receive AI-based suggestions for the best freelancers based on ratings, reviews, and budget.
  • Make secure payments and track the status of their booked services.

Service Booking & Payments

  • To confirm a booking, the customer must pay the initial travel or visit fee for the worker. This covers the worker’s travel cost to the customer’s location.
  • Once the worker reaches the customer and assesses the work, they will inform the admin of the total cost.
  • The admin will then communicate the final service price to the customer for approval.
  • The customer can then choose to proceed with:
    • A Company (official service provider)
    • A Freelancer (recommended by AI based on ratings and reviews)
    • Or continue with their already booked worker
  • The total payment will be processed through WorkHub’s secure system. WorkHub will pay the respective service provider after service completion.

Worker & Freelancer Responsibilities

  • Provide professional, high-quality, and timely service.
  • Clearly display their service range (minimum to maximum charges) on their profile.
  • Inform the admin accurately about the cost after inspection.
  • Maintain honesty, respect, and proper behavior during all jobs.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide correct details about the location, issue, and contact number.
  • Pay the initial booking or travel fee before the worker visit.
  • Respect service providers and cooperate during inspections.
  • Approve or reject the quoted price within a reasonable time frame.

AI Recommendation System

WorkHub uses Artificial Intelligence to analyze ratings, reviews, and price ranges to recommend top 5 freelancers that best fit a customer’s budget and service type. These suggestions are automated and unbiased.

Admin Rights & Verification

  • WorkHub’s admin verifies all providers before approval.
  • Admin can suspend or terminate any account that violates these Terms.
  • Admin acts as an intermediary in case of payment or service disputes.

Refunds & Disputes

Refunds are only applicable if:

  • The service provider fails to appear after booking confirmation.
  • The service was not started and cancelled before worker’s arrival.

All disputes will be resolved fairly by WorkHub’s support and admin team after reviewing all records.
